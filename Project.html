Zero Hunger Smart Agriculture Facility

Innovating agriculture for a hunger‑free world

About the Project

Our project aims to achieve Zero Hunger using an advanced smart agricultural facility that automates temperature control, lighting, nutrition, moisture, and environmental stability. Each plant receives optimized growth conditions to maximize yield while reducing waste.

How the Smart Facility Works

Using IoT sensors, automated climate systems, AI analytics, and real‑time data adjustments, the facility intelligently controls all crop growth factors. This includes:

  • Automated temperature and humidity regulation
  • Optimized sunlight or LED exposure
  • AI‑controlled nutrient delivery
  • Real‑time monitoring and reporting
